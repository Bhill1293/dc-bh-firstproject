The worlds best Knuckle Sandwich

In order to make this dish, you'll need.

  • 2 working hands
  • 1 stick of butter
  • Cheese
  • Bread (Preferably wheat)

Step 1

  • Using a butter knife,gently butter the left knuckle, from right to left
  • Let marinate for two to three minutes

Step 2

  • Place one slice of cheese evenly across the now buttered knuckle
  • Let sit

Step 3

  • Grab two slices of bread
  • butter both sides
  • Place fist between bread

VOILA!!!
